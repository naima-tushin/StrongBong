Lower back rotational stretch

The lower back rotational stretch is a simple and effective exercise for relieving tension and promoting flexibility in the lower back and hips. It's important to perform this stretch carefully and gently to avoid straining the back.

Seated lower back rotational stretch

The seated lower back rotational stretch is a variation of the lower back rotational stretch that can be done while sitting in a chair. It's a convenient way to stretch and relieve tension in your lower back, especially if you're at work or in a situation where lying on the floor is not feasible.

Office Syndrome

"Office syndrome" is a term commonly used to describe a collection of symptoms and discomfort that can result from spending long hours working in an office environment, often at a desk with a computer.
